How our
service works

Seeing that most cases of metal health disoders
bothers around financial constraints, we had come
up wit a free service for the users.

We have a free to access USSD/SHORTCODE
*713*66# that a user dials and answers 4 questions.


User responses are relayed ot our call center
Call center personnel call users to screen them further.
A free/subsidized (NHIS supported) consultation at the nearest mental unit is cliched for users who are found to require medical attention.

*Disclaimer: We neither diagnose nor offer definitive management of mental health disorders.

OUR STORY

BY FOUNDER (DR. ATSU LATEY)

"The spark... after the publication of a newspopser article on the Depression in a local newspaper " in commenmoration of World Health day in 2017, a distressed teached called the writer for help. She had identified herself as suffering from the sypmptoms he published. Fast forward, he realized that she had peculiar case of poor access to affordable mental healthcare. This case was the par excellence for many other Ghanaians. this sparked MindIT, a youth led organization which uses innovation to screen Gahanaians from psychological disress and facilitates the provision of affordable and acccessible mental healthcare to them.

THE RAFT

ABOUT THE RAFT

In the context of poor funding for mental healthcare, which is punctuated by the inacessibility and cost nature of care, it is imperative that cost-efficient soluntions are developed to address the burgeoning needs of mental healthcare in Ghana. This is one of the main aims of MindIT Ghana, as we contribute our skills pro bono to provide affordable and accesible mental healthcare for Ghanaians.
